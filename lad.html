Life’s a Drag

Life’s a Drag is a game that involves dragging to collect, combine and uncover objects. The game features various stages with unique layouts and environments, encouraging the player to think outside the box to locate items and complete each stage. The UI was designed entirely by me, and I assisted with creating stages, objects and other in-game assets. Life’s a Drag is live and available for download on the App Store and Google Play.
