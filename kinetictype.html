Kinetic Typography: "Life Isn't Just About Passing On Your Genes"

Utilizing my expertise in motion design, I developed an immersive experience using kinetic typography. Each word has been carefully planned and designed to be emblematic of the audio excerpt playing in the background. The excerpt is pulled from Metal Gear Solid 2: Sons of Liberty. The speech talks about passing on more than just our biological and genetic aspects, but our experiences and emotions as well.
