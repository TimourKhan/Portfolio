Sheikh's Pressure

Sheikh's Pressure is a pressure washing service based in the Broward County region of Florida. I was tasked with creating the branding to be used for business cards and advertisements, but most importantly the client wanted a design to be wrapped on the van. He requested a light, cartoony style that would stand out from common, corporate designs.
