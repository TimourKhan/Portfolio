White Valley Springs

White Valley Springs is a project in which I had to design the brand identity for a lodge. This lodge is branded based off of its modernity rather than being old-fashioned. It is primarily intended to be an attraction during the autumn, reflected in its color palette and imagery. Branded elements include the main logo and its variants, the front and back of a business card, the letterhead, website pages and reusable bottles.
