Right Place Right Time

Right Place Right Time is a fully functional mobile game. In this game, you play as a test subject in a simulation machine, jumping over and ducking under various obstacles while trying to avoid being frozen or burned. I created all of the game’s visuals, from the brand identity and UI to the in-game characters and backgrounds. Try it for yourself!
